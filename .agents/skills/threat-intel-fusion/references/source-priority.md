# Source Priority

Prefer sources in this order:

1. National and international agencies (CISA, NIST, ENISA, CERTs).
2. Primary annual threat reports from established security vendors.
3. Incident response reports with transparent methodology.
4. Community intelligence only when corroborated.

## Signal confidence

- High: multiple primary sources align with measurable evidence.
- Medium: single strong source, limited corroboration.
- Low: anecdotal or unverified claims.

## Do not do

- Do not treat social media claims as authoritative.
- Do not report a trend without source date and link.
- Do not provide actions that cannot be executed by a small team.
