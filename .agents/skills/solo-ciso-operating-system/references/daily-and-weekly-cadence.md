# Daily and Weekly Cadence

## Daily loop (30-60 minutes)

1. Review critical detections and overnight identity anomalies.
2. Check `P0` mission from the command queue.
3. Complete one concrete risk reduction action.
4. Log result, owner updates, and blockers.

## Weekly loop (90-120 minutes)

1. Burn down externally exploitable vulnerabilities.
2. Clear overdue remediation and expiring exceptions.
3. Refresh stale evidence and pending trust requests.
4. Update policy/control docs changed by new risks.
5. Prepare leadership one-pager: wins, residual risk, asks.

## Guardrails

- Do not leave critical exceptions unreviewed past due date.
- Do not defer identity hardening without compensating controls.
- Do not close tasks without linked evidence or verification notes.
