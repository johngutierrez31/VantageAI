# Copilot

## What this page is for

Copilot is a workflow launcher and guided reasoning layer.

It is not meant to be a generic chat interface. Its value comes from steering users into durable, reviewable workflows.

## What to ask Copilot

Ask for:

- next steps
- evidence checklists
- trust response strategy
- risk prioritization
- roadmap recommendations
- AI governance review guidance
- incident first-hour actions
- leadership summary framing

## Best prompts

Good prompts are practical and outcome-driven.

Examples:

- What should I do first for this customer diligence request?
- Turn these open findings into a 30/60/90 plan.
- What evidence do I need for access control and incident response claims?
- What conditions should we impose before approving this AI use case?
- Give me first-hour actions for a third-party breach notice.

## How to use it well

1. Ask for a concrete output
2. Review the recommended workflow links Copilot suggests
3. Open the recommended module workflow
4. Complete review, approval, and export in the underlying page

## What not to do

Do not leave important work as chat-only guidance.

If the output matters, move it into the appropriate record:

- questionnaire draft
- evidence map
- trust packet
- risk register item
- roadmap item
- AI use case review
- incident or after-action report
