# Reports, Exports, and Billing

## Reports and exports

The most important outputs in VantageAI are the artifacts you can review, send, or present.

High-value outputs include:

- questionnaire exports
- trust packets
- evidence map summaries
- Pulse board briefs
- quarterly review records
- after-action reports
- assessment reports
- weekly briefs

## How to use exports correctly

- use internal review outputs for approval and quality control
- use external or share-safe outputs for buyers, customers, or leadership
- do not export unapproved or weakly supported content
- prefer approved-only flows when trust or leadership stakes are high

## Billing and packaging

Use Billing and packaging views to understand module access and current plan framing.

Use it when you need to answer:

- what modules are included now?
- what features are premium or expansion workflows?
- where does an upgrade or packaging conversation belong?

## Best use cases

- executive packaging review
- understanding current module entitlements
- guiding expansion conversations without building billing logic into operations
