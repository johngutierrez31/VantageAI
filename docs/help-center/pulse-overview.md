# Pulse Overview

## What Pulse is for

Pulse is the recurring executive layer of VantageAI.

It turns security operations into posture scorecards, risk visibility, roadmaps, board briefs, and quarterly reviews.

## Pulse pages

- Pulse dashboard
- Pulse snapshots
- Risk register
- Roadmap
- Board briefs
- Quarterly reviews

## When to use Pulse

Use Pulse when:

- leadership wants one cyber posture view
- you need a recurring monthly or quarterly reporting rhythm
- you want a risk register tied to real operational work
- you need a 30/60/90 plan instead of a raw list of issues

## Core workflow

1. Generate a Pulse snapshot
2. Review the score and signal breakdown
3. Create or sync risks
4. Generate the roadmap
5. Prepare the board brief
6. Capture a quarterly review with decisions and follow-up

## Why it matters

Pulse is the recurring value layer of the suite. It is what makes the product useful between audits, deals, or incidents.
