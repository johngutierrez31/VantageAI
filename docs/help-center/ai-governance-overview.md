# AI Governance Overview

## What AI Governance is for

AI Governance is the module for governing AI use cases and AI vendors operationally.

Use it to make AI decisions explicit, reviewable, auditable, and connected to the rest of the suite.

## AI Governance pages

- AI Governance dashboard
- AI Use Case Registry
- AI Vendor Intake
- AI Review Queue

## Use it when

- a team wants to adopt an AI tool or workflow
- an AI vendor must be reviewed before use
- data classes, restrictions, or approval conditions need to be formalized
- AI risk needs to flow into Pulse or findings

## AI Use Case Registry

Use this page to capture:

- business owner
- team
- workflow type
- vendor or provider
- data classes involved
- review requirements
- decision conditions
- risk tier
- approval state

## AI Vendor Intake

Use this page to review:

- retention handling
- model/provider relationships
- whether customer data is used for training
- logging and audit support
- subprocessors
- DPA status
- approval conditions and blockers

## AI Review Queue

Use the queue to assign reviewers, set due dates, manage overdue approvals, and make approval decisions operational.

## Best use cases

- approving internal AI assistants
- governing customer-facing AI features
- reviewing AI SaaS vendors
- preventing shadow AI adoption from bypassing security review
