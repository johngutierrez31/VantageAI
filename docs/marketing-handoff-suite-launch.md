# Marketing Handoff: Suite Launch

## What is ready to market now

- TrustOps for buyer diligence, evidence-backed questionnaire response, review workflow, and trust packet export
- Pulse for executive scorecards, risk register, roadmap, board brief, and quarterly review
- AI Governance for use-case registry, vendor intake, approval workflow, and Pulse risk carry-over
- Response Ops for incident command, runbook task packs, after-action reporting, and tabletop preparation
- Command Center as the operating surface that ties the suite together

## Screenshots and workflows the public site should show

1. Command Center with trust, AI, incident, and executive carry-over visible at once
2. TrustOps questionnaire detail with approved answers, review-required gaps, and evidence map context
3. Trust packet view with buyer-ready export action
4. Pulse dashboard showing score, risks, roadmap, and board brief
5. AI Governance dashboard or use-case detail showing conditional approval and policy mapping
6. Response Ops incident detail with timeline, runbook tasks, and after-action follow-up

## Suggested packaging and module framing

- TrustOps: flagship entry module for procurement readiness and buyer diligence
- Pulse: executive add-on for posture visibility, risk ownership, and quarterly reporting
- AI Governance: attach module for customers operationalizing internal and customer-facing AI safely
- Response Ops: premium add-on for first-hour response, incident command, and post-incident workflow

## Suggested CTA structure

- Primary CTA: `Book a Product Demo`
- Secondary CTA: `See TrustOps in Action`
- Tertiary CTA on module pages: `See How Pulse / AI Governance / Response Ops Attach`

## Proof points to highlight

- approved answers and evidence can be reused instead of rewritten
- executive scorecards and board-ready reporting are generated inside the same app as operational workflows
- AI approvals, incidents, findings, risks, and roadmap work all connect to one operating model
- the suite is useful weekly, not just during audits or incidents

## What should not be claimed yet

- fully automated billing and packaging
- deep historical analytics or BI-grade dashboards
- full trust-center / board-book bundle packaging
- realtime SIEM or SOAR orchestration
- advanced AI runtime telemetry or red-team simulation
